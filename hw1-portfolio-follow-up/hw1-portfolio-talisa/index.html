About Me

Hi ya'll, my name is Talisa! I was born in California but grew up in the Philippines, Guam, and Texas. I recently just moved back to California, where I now live and work in San Francisco. I work at a construction technology company called Rhumbix doing all things marketing. Working for a start-up has allowed me to learn more than what my job responsibilities says on paper. When I am not working, you can find me at a yoga studio, running at one of the beautiful trails in the city, going on a road trip or traveling the world and learning about a new culture.
