About Me

Tianqi Jiang started mentoring college students after graduating from Northwestern University with a B.A. in Economics and Statistics. Working in partnership with DreambigCareer, Tianqi has been a mentor to over dozens of international students seeking jobs in the U.S. over the past three years. Through personalized one-on-one coaching sessions, Tianqi works with students through all aspects to help them get their dream offers.

Working full-time as an Economic Consultant, Tianqi has developed strong analytical and research skills. At work, she constructs financial models, analyzes quantitative data, and conducts statistical analyses to support litigation cases.

During the past six years, Tianqi has incrementally moved from the East Coast (Charlottesville, Virginia) to the West Coast (San Francisco, California) through the Midwest (Chicago, Illinois). When Tianqi is not busy planning her next westward journey (hopefully not any time soon), she likes to watch 80's and 90's sticoms (Friends, Frasier, Ellen, Seinfeld), take long walks in the city and in the nature, as well as hang out with her two cats.
